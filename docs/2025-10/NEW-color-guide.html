Key Design Decisions

✅ WCAG Compliance:
  • Primary buttons: Brand 700 (#5a7030) → 5.5:1 contrast with white
  • All text colors meet AA standards
  • Focus indicators clearly visible
⚠️ Important Distinctions:
  • --primary: For CTAs (Brand 700)
  • --brand: For brand identity (Brand 500)
  • Success green (135°) ≠ Brand green (124.5°)
Design Principles Applied:
  • 60-30-10 Rule: Neutrals (60%), Brand/Semantic (30%), Accents (10%)
  • Less is More: Reduced from 35+ to 23 core colors
  • Semantic Clarity: Each color has a single, clear purpose
  • Accessibility First: All interactive elements meet WCAG standards

Color System Summary

Final Color Count:
  • ✅ 10 Neutrals (cool undertone, perceptually uniform)
  • ✅ 5 Brand greens (all consistent hue 121.5°)
  • ✅ 12 Semantic colors (3 shades per status)
  • ✅ 2 Accent colors (purple & teal)
  • ✅ 8 Tag colors with auto contrast text
  • ✅ 5 Chart colors (linked to palette)
  • 📊 Total: 42 color definitions in OKLCH
Key Improvements:
  • ✅ 100% OKLCH color space (no hex values)
  • ✅ Success (hue 145°) vs Brand (hue 121.5°) differentiation
  • ✅ All colors WCAG AA compliant
  • ✅ Tag bg/fg pairs for automatic contrast
  • ✅ Inverted neutrals for proper dark mode
  • ✅ Chart colors aligned with semantic palette
